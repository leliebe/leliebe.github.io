Getting Started with Digital Editions

Resource List

About this page

This site is designed as a list of resources for digital humanists who are beginning to work on a digital edition. The resources provided here are by no means comprehensive, and further resources may be added over time.

If you would like to suggest resources for inclusion, or if you have questions about the site, please contact Lauren Liebe.
