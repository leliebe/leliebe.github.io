Behrend Game Jam - Spring 2025

Theme: "Beguilement"

Beguilement: to persuade, attract, or interest someone, sometimes in order to deceive them

Rules and Regulations

  • All components of the game must be created between the dates of March 22 and April 18.
  • Submissions are due by 11:59pm EST on April 18.
  • Download our submission form!
  • You must credit assets that you did not create.
  • All writing and code must be your own.
  • No generative-AI writing and no generative-AI art. AI may only be used for idea generation.
  • Your game should be fully packaged by the end.
  • There are no restrictions on game engines or software used for development.

Scoring Rubric

  • Implementation of theme
  • Aesthetics
  • Functionality
  • Creativity
  • Gameplay
