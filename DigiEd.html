Getting Started with Digital Editions

Resource List

About this page

This site is designed as a list of resources for digital humanists who are beginning to work on a digital edition. The resources provided here are by means comprehensive, and further resources may be added over time.

If you would like to suggest resources for inclusion, or if you have questions about the site, please contact Lauren Liebe.

    o A free-to-use tool hosted by 18thConnect to allow users to transcribe OCR’d versions of documents from Early English Books Online and Eighteenth-Century Collections Online. Users have full rights to publish these transcriptions.
